Who We Are

WiseMount Private Limited is a private limited company focused on enabling startups and MSMEs to build operational excellence and sustainable growth across multiple sectors.

We combine sector-specific expertise with AI-powered diagnostics and execution frameworks to deliver business development services that go beyond consulting. Our platform helps businesses identify gaps, build systems, and execute growth strategies with clarity and confidence.

We work with enterprises in healthcare, education, agribusiness, tourism, retail/D2C, and IT & AI sectors—industries that form the backbone of India's economy.
